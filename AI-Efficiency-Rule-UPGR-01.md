This rule defines a multi-stage process to maximize the efficiency of interactions with UpgradeFor generative AI.

1. **Content stage**: Clearly define the desired content before generating. Outline the scope, audience, and purpose. Use bullet points or checklists to organize information.
2. **Design stage**: Determine the presentation of the content. Consider layout, formatting, and user experience. Ensure the design matches the UpgradeFor brand guidelines.
3. **Adaptation stage**: Review generated content and adapt it to different channels or devices, ensuring readability and accessibility. Translate and localize as needed.
4. **Integration stage**: Integrate the final content into the target system (e.g., website, application, marketing materials). Test all aspects, including links and interactive elements, before publishing.

Following these stages helps ensure efficiency and quality when collaborating with AI on Upgrade Innovations projects.
